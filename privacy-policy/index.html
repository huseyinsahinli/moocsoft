Privacy Policy

Application: NutriLens: Food AI Scanner · Developer: Moocsoft · Last Updated: December 8, 2024

1. Introduction

Moocsoft ("we," "our," or "us"), operated by Hüseyin Şahinli, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our food analysis application, NutriLens: Food AI Scanner ("the App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 User Authentication Data

  • Firebase Anonymous Authentication ID: A unique identifier created automatically when you first use the App
  • Purpose: This ID is used solely to manage your credit balance in our system
  • Note: This is not linked to any personal information, email, phone number, or identity

2.2 Credit Usage Data

  • Credit Balance: Stored in Firebase Firestore
  • Transaction History: Records of credit deductions when you use the analysis service
  • Purpose: To manage and track your service usage

2.3 Food Photos

  • Source: Photos taken from your camera or selected from your gallery
  • Processing: Photos are sent to Google Gemini API for analysis
  • Storage: We do not permanently store your photos on our servers
  • Retention: Photos are only temporarily processed and immediately discarded after analysis

2.4 Analysis Results

  • Data: Nutritional information and analysis results returned from Gemini API
  • Display: Results are shown within the App
  • Storage: Results may be temporarily stored for display purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide food analysis functionality
  • Credit Management: To track and manage your credit balance
  • Service Improvement: To improve the accuracy and quality of our analysis
  • Technical Maintenance: To maintain and optimize App performance

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We Do NOT Share Your Information

  • We do not sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties
  • We do not share your data with advertisers or marketing companies
  • Your authentication ID and credit information remain confidential

4.2 Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

Google Firebase

  • Services Used: Firebase Authentication, Firebase Firestore
  • Data Processed: Anonymous user ID, credit balance
  • Purpose: User authentication and data storage
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Google Gemini API

  • Data Processed: Food photos you upload
  • Purpose: AI-powered food analysis
  • Retention: Photos are processed in real-time and not permanently stored by us
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., court orders, government agencies).
